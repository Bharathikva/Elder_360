About Us

Elders Helping is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing support and assistance to elderly individuals in our community. We believe that every elderly person deserves to live a happy and healthy life, and we work to make that possible by providing a range of services and resources.

Our Services

  • Companionship
  • Meal preparation and delivery
  • Transportation to appointments and errands
  • Housekeeping and home maintenance
  • Assistance with personal care
